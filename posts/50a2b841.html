欧姆定律

R =UI\dfrac{U}{I}IU​ ; I =UR\dfrac{U}{R}RU​ ; U = IR

串联与并联各电流、电压、电阻公式

串联

串联总电流I:I=II = II=I

串联总电阻R:Rtotal=R1+R2.....+RnR_{total} = R_1 + R_2 ..... + R_nRtotal​=R1​+R2​.....+Rn​

串联总电压U:U=U1+U2.......+UnU = U_1 + U_2....... + U_nU=U1​+U2​.......+Un​

电阻R求导过程:

U=IR=IR1+IR2+IRn=I(R1+R2+......+Rn) \quad\quad\quad \begin{aligned} \quad\quad\quad U &= IR = IR_1 + IR_2 + IR_n \\ \quad\quad\quad &= I(R_1 + R2 + ...... + Rn) \\ \end{aligned} U​=IR=IR1​+IR2​+IRn​=I(R1​+R2+......+Rn)​

串联的分压公式:

Un=RnR1+...RnU\quad\quad\quad \quad\quad\quad U_n = \dfrac{R_n}{R_1 + ... R_n}U \quad\quad\quadUn​=R1​+...Rn​Rn​​U

推导过程:

根据欧姆定律
∵I=UR \because I=\dfrac{U}{R} \quad∵I=RU​

∴Un=URTRn \therefore U_n =\dfrac{U}{R_T}R_n ∴Un​=RT​U​Rn​

∵ \because ∵串联电阻
RT=R1+R2+...RnR_T = R_1+R_2+...R_n\quadRT​=R1​+R2​+...Rn​

∴Un=UR1+...RnRn也可以写成=RnR1+...RnU \begin{aligned} \therefore U_n =& \dfrac{U}{R_1 + ... R_n}R_n \\ \text{也可以写成}\quad\quad =& \dfrac{R_n}{R_1 + ... R_n}U \\ \end{aligned} ∴Un​=也可以写成=​R1​+...Rn​U​Rn​R1​+...Rn​Rn​​U​

计算过程:

Un=IRn=URTRn=UR1+...RnRn=RnR1+...RnU\begin{aligned} U_n &= IR_n \\ &=\dfrac{U}{R_T}R_n \\ &=\dfrac{U}{R_1 + ... R_n}R_n \\ &=\dfrac{R_n}{R_1 + ... R_n}U \\ \end{aligned} Un​​=IRn​=RT​U​Rn​=R1​+...Rn​U​Rn​=R1​+...Rn​Rn​​U​

并联

并联总电流I:I=I1+I2+......InI = I_1 + I_2 + ...... I_nI=I1​+I2​+......In​
并联总电压U:U=U1=U2=UnU = U_1 = U_2 = U_nU=U1​=U2​=Un​

并联总电阻R:RT=11R1+1R2+......1RnR_T = \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{1}{R_1}+ \dfrac{1}{R_2} + ......\dfrac{1}{R_n}}RT​=R1​1​+R2​1​+......Rn​1​1​

也可写=R1R2...RnR2Rn+R1Rn+R1R2={\dfrac{R_1R_2...R_n}{R_2R_n + R_1R_n + R_1R_2}}=R2​Rn​+R1​Rn​+R1​R2​R1​R2​...Rn​​


R1R2...RnR2Rn+R1Rn+R1R2{\dfrac{R_1R_2...R_n}{R_2R_n + R_1R_n + R_1R_2}}R2​Rn​+R1​Rn​+R1​R2​R1​R2​...Rn​​ 的计算过程:

RT=11R1+1R2+......1Rn通分得:=1R2Rn+R1Rn+R1R2R1R2...Rn取倒数得:=1∗R1R2...RnR2Rn+R1Rn+R1R2最后得到:=R1R2...RnR2Rn+R1Rn+R1R2 \quad\quad\quad\quad \quad\quad\quad\quad \begin{aligned} R_T &= \dfrac{1}{\dfrac{1}{R_1}+ \dfrac{1}{R_2} + ......\dfrac{1}{R_n}} \\ \text{通分得:}\quad\quad\quad\quad &=\dfrac{1}{\dfrac{R2R_n + R_1R_n + R_1R_2}{R_1R_2...R_n}} \\ \text{取倒数得:}\quad\quad\quad\quad &=1 * {\dfrac{R_1R_2...R_n}{R2R_n + R_1R_n + R_1R_2}} \\ \text{最后得到:}\quad\quad\quad\quad &=\dfrac{R_1R_2...R_n}{R2R_n + R_1R_n + R_1R_2} \\ \end{aligned} RT​通分得:取倒数得:最后得到:​=R1​1​+R2​1​+......Rn​1​1​=R1​R2​...Rn​R2Rn​+R1​Rn​+R1​R2​​1​=1∗R2Rn​+R1​Rn​+R1​R2​R1​R2​...Rn​​=R2Rn​+R1​Rn​+R1​R2​R1​R2​...Rn​​​

电阻R求导过程:

∵I=I1+I2+......In根据I=UR  =U1R1+U2R3+...UnRn∵并联电路电压相等∴  =U(1R1+1R2...+1Rn)根据R=UI得:  =UU(1R1+1R2...+1Rn)同时除以U得:  =11R1+1R2...+1Rn \quad\quad\quad\quad \quad\quad\quad\quad \begin{aligned} \because I &= I_1 + I_2 + ...... I_n \\ \text{根据}I=\dfrac{U}{R}\quad\quad\quad\quad\ \ &=\dfrac{U_1}{R_1} +\dfrac{U_2}{R_3} + ... \dfrac{U_n}{R_n} \\ \because \text{并联电路电压相等}\therefore\ \ \quad\quad\quad\quad &=U(\dfrac{1}{R_1} + \dfrac{1}{R_2} ... + \dfrac{1}{R_n}) \\ \text{根据} R = \dfrac{U}{I} \text{得:}\ \quad\quad\quad\quad\ &=\dfrac{U}{U(\dfrac{1}{R_1} + \dfrac{1}{R_2} ... + \dfrac{1}{R_n})} \\ \text{同时除以}U\text{得:} \ \quad\quad\quad\quad\ &=\dfrac{1}{\dfrac{1}{R_1} + \dfrac{1}{R_2} ... + \dfrac{1}{R_n}} \\ \end{aligned} ∵I根据I=RU​  ∵并联电路电压相等∴  根据R=IU​得:  同时除以U得:  ​=I1​+I2​+......In​=R1​U1​​+R3​U2​​+...Rn​Un​​=U(R1​1​+R2​1​...+Rn​1​)=U(R1​1​+R2​1​...+Rn​1​)U​=R1​1​+R2​1​...+Rn​1​1​​
